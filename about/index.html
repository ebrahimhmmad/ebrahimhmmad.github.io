Ebrahim Jehad Hmmad

An architecture student with a passion for capturing stories through design, photography, and art.

My journey began with love for form and function, but it quickly expanded into a multidisciplinary pursuit of creativity. As an aspiring architect, I'm driven by a desire to shape spaces that not only look beautiful but also feel meaningful. My academic work explores the relationship between structure and emotion, blending practical design with artistic expression.

Beyond architecture, I'm a photographer who sees the world in light, contrast, and moments. My camera is a tool to document not just places, but people, moods, and stories often influenced by my architectural eye for detail, geometry, and texture.

As an artist, I enjoy experimenting with mediums, from sketching and digital art to conceptual installations. My work is often inspired by the intersection of urban life, nature, and human experience. I'm constantly learning, evolving, and expanding my skills. Whether it's graphic design, model-making, storytelling, or digital rendering, I'm always eager to take on new creative challenges.

Thanks for stopping by feel free to explore my work and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate.
